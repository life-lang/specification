Actors are abstractions which perform work (process).

The most basic definition of an Actor is:

<ActorName> (as actor)
	[public interface]
		services:
			[dependencies]
			[msgs]
	[internals]
		attributes:
		invariants:
		methods:

About '[public interface]' section.

'services' subsection contains operations those the actor can perform.

About '[internals]' section.

'attributes' subsection contains attributes definitions about the actor.
'invariants' subsection contains logical expressions applied on attributes of the actor.
'methods' subsection contains operations those the actor can perform.
